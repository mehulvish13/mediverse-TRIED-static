About Mediverse+

Learn about our AI-powered health assistance platform and the technology behind it.

Our Mission

Mediverse+ is dedicated to making healthcare more accessible through artificial intelligence. Our platform uses advanced machine learning algorithms to analyze symptoms and provide preliminary health insights, helping users make informed decisions about their health.

Technology Behind Mediverse+

Machine Learning

Our system uses Support Vector Machine (SVM) algorithms trained on medical symptom data to classify and predict potential health conditions.

Natural Language Processing

Advanced NLP techniques including TF-IDF vectorization allow our AI to understand complex symptom descriptions in natural language.
