Privacy Policy

How we protect and handle your medical information

Last Updated: August 29, 2025

Privacy Overview

Key Point

Mediverse+ is designed with privacy-first principles. We do not collect, store, or share personal identifying information. Your symptom descriptions are processed temporarily for AI analysis and then automatically deleted.

What This Privacy Policy Covers

This Privacy Policy explains how Mediverse+ ("we," "our," or "us") handles information when you use our AI-powered symptom checker service. This policy applies to all users of our web-based application.

Information We Collect
What We DO Collect
  • Symptom Descriptions: The text you provide describing your symptoms
  • Technical Data: Browser type, IP address (anonymized), device type for security and performance
  • Usage Analytics: Anonymous usage statistics to improve our service
What We DON'T Collect
  • Personal Identity: No names, addresses, phone numbers, or email addresses
  • Medical Records: We don't create or store medical records
  • Account Information: No user accounts or login requirements
  • Payment Data: We don't collect financial information (service is free)
How We Use Information
AI Analysis

Your symptom descriptions are processed by our AI model to generate health predictions

Service Improvement

Anonymous usage data helps us improve accuracy and user experience

Security & Safety

Technical data helps us maintain security and prevent misuse

Data Storage & Security
Temporary Processing

Symptom data is processed temporarily and automatically deleted after analysis completion

SSL Encryption

All data transmission is protected using industry-standard SSL/TLS encryption

Secure Servers

Our servers are protected with advanced security measures and regular updates

No Personal Profiles

We don't create user profiles or track individual users across sessions

Your Rights & Control
What You Can Do
  • Use Anonymously: No registration or personal information required
  • Control Information: You choose what symptom information to provide
  • Clear Data: Close your browser to clear any temporary session data
  • Contact Us: Ask questions about our privacy practices anytime
Our Commitments
  • No Selling: We never sell or rent your information
  • No Sharing: Your data is not shared with third parties
  • No Tracking: We don't track you across other websites
  • Transparency: Clear information about our practices
Legal Compliance
Healthcare Privacy Standards

While Mediverse+ is not a covered entity under HIPAA (Health Insurance Portability and Accountability Act), we follow HIPAA-inspired privacy practices to protect your health information. Our service is designed as an educational tool and does not create doctor-patient relationships or medical records.

Data Protection

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. However, no internet transmission is 100% secure, so we cannot guarantee absolute security.
